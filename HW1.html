A list of my top 5 favourite movies!

1. The Godfather Part 2

This is the second entry to the Brilliant Francis Ford Coppola's cinematic magnum opus known as The Godfather Trilogy. It stars The legendary actors Marlon Brando, Al Pacino, and Robert De Niro. It is widely acclaimed as one of if not the best on-screen portrayals of the Italian American mob in cinema, as well as one of the most critically acclaimed films in history.

2. Scott Pilgrim vs The World

Directed by Edgar Wright, starring Michael Cera and Mary Elizabeth Winstead, I would describe this movie as pure fun. If a videogame was a movie, this would be it. It's about the title character Scott and his quest to win the heart of the new girl in town Ramona, as he battles her leaugue of 7 evil exes, which he must defeat to date her. This movie encapsulates the conflicts one endures as a young adult trying to find love and the challenges that come with it, while still maintaning a constant pace of upbeat action sequences and great comedic timing.

3. Midnight in Paris

This is a heartwarming movie directd by Woody Allen, starring household names Owen Wilson and Rachel McAdams. It is about an author currently unhappy in his life, career, and relationship, consumed with nostalgia, and with dreams of standing amongst his literary heroes...a dream which comes true in a strange way while on vacation with his fiance. Despite the somber unerlying tone, I really enjoyed the dialogue, message, and comedy of the movie.

4. Before Trilogy (Sunrise, Sunset, Midnight)

As someone who typically doesn't enjoy the rom-com format, I have to say that this particular movie blew my mind. With long yet continuously engaging dialogue filmed over a single take, this Richard Linklater series of movies follows the relationship and growth of two strangers, incredibly played by Ethan Hawke and Julie Delpy, falling in and out of love over decades.

5. Inception

While known for his signature plot twists, I believe that this movie cemented Christopher Nolan's legacy as a director ability to interweave grandious cinematic sequences with the most mind-boggling and intricate concepts imaginable. Starring the sensational Leonardo DiCaprio it follows a group of mind thieves attempting to steal a valuable piece of information from within other people's dreams, where all logic is thrown out the window, making opportunites of physics defying stunts and sequences.
